Queen Mary Centre of Undergraduate Research

My work at the Queen Mary Centre of Undergraduate Research involved me working on a data collection project. This project focused on the effects of ageing populations on life in the United Kingdom, exploring multiple aspects, such as policymaking, technological advancements, accident rates, and healthcare.

Open the full report in a new tab ↗

The process involved going through published literature that is relevant to the topic of interest, and then finding and extracting data that would be considered important to note for the purposes of the project. This can include demographic data, details about recently-innovated technology, and studies that look into how different age population groups have differing healthcare issues. I presented my findings at Queen Mary University of London's Industrial Liaison Forum to 100+ faculty professors and industry representatives.
